Year of a Butterfly is a multi-disciplinary art project consisting of Film, Poetry, Paintings, Music and Culinary pieces.

Year of a Butterfly was created by applying an artistic method we call "Live. Feel. Harvest." The concept consists of placing onself in a prolonged states of unusual living conditions with the goal of stirring feelings and emotions that are ordinarily rarely or never reached. Psychologically unbalanced states reached by this exercise are "harvested" and shaped into various art forms that spontaeiously emerge throoughout the experience.
